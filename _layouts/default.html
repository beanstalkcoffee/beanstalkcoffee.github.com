Beanstalk provides the ultimate bespoke coffee experience at events and outdoor locations across the UK.

For us, it's about serving directly traded speciality coffee with verve and panache for your guests to savour and enjoy.

The delivery of great coffee comes down to people. The founder of Beanstalk Shaun Young, is the UK Aeropress Champion and is accompanied by a selection of the UK's best baristas who are the heart and soul of many of London's great coffee bars.

Beanstalk operates a bespoke hand-crafted espresso bar where our ethos is built on providing a unique coffee experience that matches the UK's leading artisan cafes.

Working with our well sourced suppliers our philosophy is totally dedicated to uncompromising quality.

We started this venture with the aim of delivering the best possible coffee experience at outdoor events and conferences. Great time and consideration has been invested throughout, enabling us to provide a dynamic, young and passionate company.

Hospitality

Wherever you need excellent coffee, Beanstalk can deliver. At conferences, corporate hospitality events, private functions or wherever you choose.

We provide a fully stocked and professional barista service that far exceeds most cafés in terms of the quality product and customer service.

Beanstalk supplies you with everything to serve a full menu of directly traded speciality coffees plus a selection of loose leaf tea’s and hot chocolate.

We are equipped with state of the art mobile coffee equipment, allowing us to serve coffee at high volume with speed and efficiency.

Beanstalk looks forward to delivering exceptional coffee to your clients. Please contact us with your requirements.
