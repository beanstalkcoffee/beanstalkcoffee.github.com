Beanstalk provides the ultimate bespoke coffee experience at events and outdoor locations across the UK.

For us, it's about serving directly traded speciality coffees with verve and panache for your guests to savour and enjoy.

The delivery of great coffee comes down to people. The founder of Beanstalk Shaun Young, is the UK Aeropress Champion and is accompanied by a selection of the UK's best baristas who are the heart and soul of many of London's great coffee bars.

Beanstalk operates a bespoke hand-crafted espresso bar where our ethos is built on providing a unique coffee experience that matches the UK's leading artisan cafes.

Working with our well sourced suppliers our philosophy is totally dedicated to uncompromising quality.

Suppliers

Beanstalk is built on providing the finest possible products. We as a company are incredibly proud to work and sustain great relationships with our suppliers.

Has Bean

Has Bean Coffee are a small speciality roastery focussed on sourcing incredible coffee and roasting with great care to deliver the best possible cup. Sourcing the majority of their coffees direct from individual farmers their key values are honesty, provenance, freshness and great flavour – they are proud to have their coffee brewed by some of the most passionate and skilled coffee people in the world.

Rare Tea Company

Rare Tea Company is small, independent company based in London.

The company was founded in 2004, by Henrietta Lovell (known as the Tea-Lady) to source and supply the world's best tea - direct from farmers and their tea gardens.

From humble beginnings with just a handful of devoted customers, they now collaborate with many of the world finest chefs and restaurateurs.

Ivy House Farm

Having started farming in the lovely Somerset village of Beckington in the early eighties, they now milk 120 Jersey cows, rear all there calves and aim to grow all the feed for the animals.

Everybody in the family is involved in the business from taking orders, milking the cows, packaging products to delivering the milk and cream.

Processing all there own milk products has allowed the small farm to survive. By offering this organic jersey milk they are supporting the countryside, its community, the animals and their environment

People

Shaun Young

Founder

With experience in Michelin starred restaurants, British gastro pubs and speciality cafe’s both in the UK and Australia, Shaun’s understanding of the hospitality industry is extensive.

Through a culmination of knowledge, capability and three years dedication Shaun became the UK Aeropress Champion in 2012, going on to represent the UK at the World AeropressChampionships in Portland, Oregon. Shaun is the current Head Barista of Kaffeine, one of the UK’s leading artisan cafes.

Ben Drury

Barista

With 5 years in the industry, Ben is regarded as one of the UK's best baristas. Claire Brice

Pete Garcia

Barista
