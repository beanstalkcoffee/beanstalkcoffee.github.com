Beanstalk provides the ultimate bespoke coffee experience at events and outdoor locations across the UK.

For us, it's about serving directly traded speciality coffees with verve and panache for your guests to savour and enjoy.

The delivery of great coffee comes down to people. The founder of Beanstalk Shaun Young, is the Uk Aeropress Champion and is accompanied by a selection of the UK's best baristas who are the heart and soul of many of London's great coffee bars.

Beanstalk operates a bespoke hand-crafted espresso bar where our ethos is built on providing a unique coffee experience that matches the UK's leading artisan cafes.

Working with our well sourced suppliers our philosophy is totally dedicated to uncompromising quality
