Beanstalk provides the best possible coffee experience at events and outdoor locations across the UK. For us it’s not just about being a coffee outlet, it’s about delivering the passion around it. We offer directly traded speciality coffee, served with panache that guests of events and markets will savour!

The Deliverance of a great product comes down to people. Beanstalk’s award winning team is built from a pool of some of the UK’s best barista’s that are the heart and soul in many of the infamous London Espresso bars, and include the founder of Beanstalk, Shaun Young who is the 2012 UK Aeropress Champion.

Beanstalks’ operates a bespoke hand-crafted espresso bar and is equipped with some of the best equipment on today’s current market.

Our philosophy is total uncompromising quality, and that means we only work with our well sourced suppliers, as well as continuously looking for new exciting coffee developments.

Our ethos is built on providing a unique coffee experience that matches the UK's leading artisan cafes.
